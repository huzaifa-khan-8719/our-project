COVID Viruses: A Brief Overview COVID viruses belong to the coronavirus family, known for causing respiratory illnesses ranging from mild colds to severe diseases. The most notable is SARS-CoV-2, responsible for the COVID-19 pandemic. These viruses spread through respiratory droplets and can cause symptoms like fever, cough, and difficulty breathing.

protect now

take steps to protect yourself

Wear A Face Mask

Wearing a mask during COVID helps prevent the spread of respiratory droplets that may contain the virus. It protects both the wearer and others, especially in crowded or indoor settings.

learn more

Wash Your Hands

Washing your hands helps remove viruses and prevent the spread of COVID-19. It reduces the risk of infection by eliminating germs you might pick up from surfaces.

learn more

Avoid Close Contact

Avoid close contact during COVID-19 to reduce the risk of virus transmission through respiratory droplets. Staying apart minimizes the chances of inhaling or touching surfaces contaminated with the virus.

learn more

What Are The Main Symptoms?

The main symptoms of COVID-19 are fever, cough, fatigue, sore throat, loss of taste or smell, and difficulty breathing.
