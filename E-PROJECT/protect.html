Protect Yourself from COVID-19

Follow these guidelines to stay safe:

  • Wash your hands regularly with soap and water.
  • Maintain social distancing (at least 6 feet apart).
  • Wear a mask in crowded places.
  • Avoid touching your face, especially eyes, nose, and mouth.
  • Stay home if you feel unwell.

Get vaccinated and stay updated with booster doses.

Disinfect frequently touched surfaces daily.

Boost your immune system with a healthy diet and exercise.
