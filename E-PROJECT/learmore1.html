Wear a Mask, Stay Safe

Protect yourself and others from COVID-19.

Why Wear a Mask?

Wearing a mask helps prevent the spread of COVID-19, especially in crowded places.

Mask-Wearing Tips

  • Ensure your mask covers your nose and mouth.
  • Wash your hands before and after touching your mask.
  • Use a clean mask every day.
  • Avoid touching the front of the mask while wearing it.
